Mr. Narendra Pandya

(Owner & founder of Raj Laxmi Enterprises,
SalesPromoter of double bull cement)

Raj Cement Articles was established by Mr.Narendra Pandya in the year 1995. The factory was initially setup for manufacturing block and tiles(mojak). Gradually, factory has undergone through various upgradations and started manufacturing pewar blocks. Later, Mr. Narendra Pandya achieved wonder cement dealership and Raj Cement Articles got renamed to Raj Laxmi Enterprises. Under him, the factory supplied wonder cement for more than 8 years. On July 28, Mr. Narendra Pandya became SalesPromoter under double bull cement, since then up to the current day, factory is supplying doublebull cements and pewar blocks.
